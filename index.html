Project Eco08

Supported by the Jeju island's Council of Sustainable Development, this project aims to harmonize between residents (including foreign visitors) and the breathtaking nature of Jeju's Island by digitizing a widely used local map that promotes sustainable development practices. By mainly focusing on the 3R principles (reduce, reuse and recycle), work with over 100 independent service providers all over Jeju Island to create a community engaging platform.

User-Interative Dashboard

The dashboard allows users to search for information about eco-friendly places in Jeju. Users can enter one of the 60 places in the search box and view its details along with accompanying graphs. The "Most Frequent Word" section displays keywords that appear most frequently in blogs related to the chosen place, providing an overview of its content. The "Rating of the place" section indicates the overall rating and atmosphere based on the ratio of positive, neutral, and negative comments. Additionally, the "Frequency of the Neutral, Positive Sentiment" section highlights keywords found in the "Neutral" and "Positive" rated blogs, offering a quick understanding of the place's pros and cons.
